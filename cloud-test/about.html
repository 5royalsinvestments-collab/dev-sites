How It Started

A Kitchen Roaster & a Big Idea

It started in 2017 with a cast-iron skillet, a bag of green beans from Ethiopia, and a stubborn belief that Yukon deserved world-class coffee without the big-city attitude.

Founders Mae & Dale Hargrove spent two years tinkering, failing, and finally dialing in roast profiles that made their neighbors show up uninvited on roast days just to smell the smoke.

In 2019, they opened the doors at 114 W Main St. The line was out the door on day one and it hasn't stopped since.
