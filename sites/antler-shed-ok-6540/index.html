Questions

Frequently Asked

Is it really free to join?
Yes — free membership is genuinely free. You'll get immediate access to the drop timeline, scouting guides, and the community. We may offer premium resources in the future, but core membership stays free.
Do I need to be an experienced shed hunter?
Not at all. We have guides specifically built for beginners who've never found a shed and veterans who want to find even more. Everyone is welcome.
Is this only for public land hunters?
No. Our scouting principles, drop timelines, and community apply to both public land and private property hunters across Oklahoma.
Do I need an antler dog?
Nope. Plenty of members hunt on foot without a dog and find great sheds. Dog training content is just one of many resources — use what applies to you.
How do I contact you?
Reach us anytime at info@antlershedok.com — we typically reply within 24 hours.
